Survey and Focus Group

In order to better understand the feelings and perspectives of the client's retired employees, our team conducted a survey as well as a focus group on the topic of pension statements and how to best deliver information to retired employees in the future.

Challenge

The client was keen to have a clear understanding of how the current publications, as well as the design, production and delivery of the annual pension statement were serving the retiree population.

Solution

In order to explore the possibility of an online portal in the future, the survey included questions to collect opinions and preferences around changes in the delivery of information via different technologies.

I wrote and prepared this report for the client using the information collected from the survey, as well as the focus group meeting. All information specific to the client and any participants has been removed.
