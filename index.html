Hi! I'm Erin.

I am a front end developer based in Toronto. I love languages (for speaking and for coding), and I love to build and create things - on the computer and in real life. Seeing a project come together is the best feeling, and is what constantly drives me forward.

Because I have worn many hats in the past, I am able to bring my experience in graphic design, building great user experiences through communications, and quality control via project management to deliver a pixel-perfect site, with meticulous attention to the finer details.
